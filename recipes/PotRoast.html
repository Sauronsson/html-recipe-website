Hannah's Pot Roast


Ingredients

  • 1 lb carrots
  • 2 potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 lb chuck roast
  • 1/4 cup water

Recipe

  1. First, start by preparing all the ingredients. Chop the carrots and potatoes into bite sized pieces. Mince the onions
  2. Season the vegetables heftily
  3. Add everything into the crockpot
  4. Let cook for 6 hours on medium. Towards the end of the cooking session you should taste the vegetables to make sure they are seasoned appropriately.
