Ground Turkey and Broccoli Pasta


Ingredients

  • 3/4 pound ground turkey
  • 2 cups pasta gluten-free
  • 1/2 onion chopped
  • 1 head broccoli
  • 2 medium tomatoes diced
  • 2 tsp mustard
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp olive oil

Recipe

  1. Cook the pasta: i.e. add water to pot. Enough salt that it tastes like the ocean, add pasta and let sit until al dente.
  2. Preheat large skillet, drizzle olive oil, Add ground turkey and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add chopped onions to turkey, and cook until onions are translucent (~ 5 minutes)
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, broccoli, mustard, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Cook until broccoli is soft and there is a light sauce on the bottom of the pan.
  5. Combine pasta with turkey sauce in a large bowl. SERVE!
